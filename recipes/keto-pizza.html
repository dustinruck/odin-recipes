Keto Pizza

Description

This keto-friendly pizza is sure to be a hit! It's crust is a simple low-carb tortilla topped with pepperoni, mozzarella and any other low-carb toppings you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla Factory Tortilla
  • 19 Slices Mike's Pepperoni
  • 1 Tbsp Walden Farms Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella
  • Olives, Sausage, Smoked Meat... (to your taste)

Steps

  1. Spread the tomato sauce on the tortilla.
  2. Spread the 19 slices of pepperoni evenly over the crust.
  3. Spread the mozzarella evenly across the crust.
  4. Add any other toppings you may like
  5. Bake in the over at 400 for 12 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
